Welcome to the Frog Compendium: A Comprehensive Guide to Frogs

Within these digital pages, you will find enlightenment regarding the noble endeavors of the Blue Frog Protectors Guild (BFPG), dedicated to the preservation and safeguarding of our amphibious allies. In symbiosis we dwell alongside our frog brethren. However, a somber shadow now looms over our shared lands as a malevolent group has entered our kingdom of Afrogtasia.

The invasion of the Cane toads has disrupted the peace of Afrogtasia, civil unrest brews as tensions rise between the amphibians. In response to this dire threat, the BFPG swiftly mobilizes to fortify our defenses and repel the approaching war party.

Amidst escalating tensions within the frog kingdom, His Majesty King Frogalishious has decreed a state of emergency. The insidious Toad Inquisition launches a systematic campaign, ravaging both habitat and populace, compelling the king to beseech the BFPG for aid in safeguarding our cherished kingdom.

Should you elect to embark upon this noble quest, your mission shall be one of paramount importance: to champion the cause of protection and preservation. Through the utilization of this digital repository, you shall acquaint yourself with the myriad species and settlements that adorn our beloved land. By arming yourself with knowledge and employing diligent conservation efforts, you shall serve as a bastion of hope amidst the encroaching darkness.
