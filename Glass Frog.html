Glass Frog

The glass frog, belonging to the Centrolenidae family, is renowned for its translucent skin, allowing a clear view of its internal organs. These delicate creatures inhabit the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

Subspecies and Colorations

    Greenhouse Frog:
  • Known for its bright green color and transparent abdomen.

Glass frogs are typically found near streams and rivers where they lay their eggs on leaves overhanging the water. Their diet consists mainly of small insects and spiders.
