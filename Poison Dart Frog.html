The Poison Dart Frog

Poison dart frogs, members of the Dendrobatidae family, are known for their bright colours and potent toxins. These small, vividly coloured frogs are native to Central and South America

Subspecies and Colorations

    Blue Poison Dart Frog:
  • Bright blue with black spots.
    • Strawberry Poison Dart Frog:
  • Red body with blue legs, also known as the "blue jeans frog."
    • Golden Poison Dart Frog:
  • Bright yellow or orange, one of the most toxic animals on Earth.

Despite their toxicity, poison dart frogs are relatively small, with adults typically measuring around 1.5 cm to 6 cm in length. Their toxic mucus coating helps deter potential predators from eating them. They feed on ants, termites, and other small insects.
