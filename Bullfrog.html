Bullfrog

The bullfrog, also known as Lithobates catesbeianus, is one of the largest and most formidable members of the frog family. Known for its powerful legs and deep, resonant call, the bullfrog is a true symbol of strength in the frog world.

Subspecies and Colorations

  • American Bullfrog:

    Typically green to brown with dark blotches and a white or yellow belly.

Bullfrogs are highly adaptable and can be found in various habitats ranging from ponds and lakes to marshes and swamps. Their diet includes insects, small fish, and even other frogs.
